# About me

    Alessandro Sebastianelli graduated with laude in Electronic Engineering for Automation and Telecommunications at the University of Sannio in October 2019. He is enrolled in the Ph.D. programme with University of Sannio, and his research topics mainly focus on Remote Sensing and Satellite data analysis, Artificial Intelligence techniques for Earth Observation, data fusion and Quantum Artificial Intelligence for Earth Observation. He has been a visiting researcher at Phi-lab in European Space Research Institute (ESRIN) of the European Space Agency (ESA), in Frascati, and still collaborates with the Phi-lab. He has won an ESA OSIP proposal in August 2020 presented with his Ph.D. Supervisor, Prof. Silvia L. Ullo.
